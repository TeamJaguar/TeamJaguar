Intercollege Robo Soccer

The Robo Soccer competition is an exhilarating event where robotics enthusiasts showcase their skills in designing and programming robotic soccer players. Participants create autonomous robots capable of dribbling, passing, and scoring goals in a fast-paced soccer match. This competition challenges teams to combine engineering prowess and AI algorithms to outmaneuver their opponents and claim victory on the soccer field. It's not just about winning; it's a platform for innovation, teamwork, and the sheer thrill of robotic sportsmanship.

Intercollege Robo Sumo

The Robo Sumo competition is an intense battle of engineering and strategy, where robotic creations face off in a sumo wrestling-style showdown. These compact yet powerful robots are designed to push their opponents out of a circular ring. Precision, agility, and brute force collide as teams compete to build the most formidable sumo robot. It's a test of both technical prowess and tactical finesse, where participants aim to outmaneuver and overpower their rivals, all within the confines of the sumo arena. The Robo Sumo competition offers an exciting glimpse into the future of robotic combat.
