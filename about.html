About Us

Our History

Formed 8 years ago by Shridhar Balgude, Rahul Pardeshi, and Surender Jatt, our robotics club has grown and evolved over the years.

Our Team

Currently, we have 8 dedicated members actively contributing to our robotics endeavors.

Our Journey

We've delved deeper into the world of robotics year by year, honing our skills and knowledge.

Our Activities

Alongside participating in robotics events and competitions, we conduct seminars, workshops, and lectures for students, aiming to demystify the term 'robotics' and inspire future innovators.

Our Achievements

We are proud to be recognized as Team #1 in Pune. In collaboration with different institutions, we conduct lecture series for students. We have achieved unbeatable records in prestigious institutions such as IIT Indore, MANIT Bhopal, JECRC Jaipur, RTU Rajasthan, IIT Delhi, IIT Bombay, IIT Kanpur, and IIT Guwahati, among many others.
